My Engineering Journey

My passion for electronics, robotics, and intelligent systems led me to explore projects that blend hardware, software, and real-world problem-solving. Over time, this curiosity grew into a strong interest in autonomous vehicles and advanced control systems.

I have worked on building an Autonomous Baja Vehicle, where I developed the ACC algorithm, integrated radar and IMU sensors, worked with CAN communication, and tested autonomous behavior in MATLAB and RoadRunner. Along with this, I gained experience across multiple domains such as PCB design, embedded systems, mechanical design, ANSYS analysis, and robotics.

Engineering, for me, is about creating solutions that work in the real world—whether it’s an autonomous vehicle, a robotic system, or an innovative device. Every project I take on pushes me to learn more, improve my skills, and move closer to becoming a versatile engineer.
